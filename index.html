About

Applied linguistics and English-medium higher education

Sihan Zhou formerly served as Tutor of Applied Linguistics at the University of Oxford, Lecturer at CUHK English Language Teaching Unit, and TESOL Associate at the University of Edinburgh.

As a teacher educator, Sihan Zhou has taught pre- and in-service language teachers at primary, secondary, and tertiary levels, and delivered training workshops in China and the UK.

Research

Research themes

Self-regulated language learning

Listening, motivation, metacognitive strategies, self-efficacy, and learner agency in transition to English-medium higher education.

English medium instruction (EMI)

Language support, EMI policy and practice, student participation, and academic communication in multilingual university settings.

Technology-assisted language learning

Mobile-assisted self-regulated listening, AI-supported language learning, digital dialogue journaling, and online learning communities.
