About

Sihan Zhou

Assistant Professor of Curriculum and Instruction at The Chinese University of Hong Kong.

Sihan Zhou is an Assistant Professor of Curriculum and Instruction at The Chinese University of Hong Kong. Sihan Zhou completed her DPhil in Education at the University of Oxford and an MPhil at the University of Cambridge.

Sihan Zhou formerly served as Tutor of Applied Linguistics at the University of Oxford, Lecturer at CUHK English Language Teaching Unit, and TESOL Associate at the University of Edinburgh. Her research focuses on self-regulated language learning, English medium instruction, and technology-assisted language learning.

As a teacher educator, Sihan Zhou has taught pre- and in-service language teachers at primary, secondary, and tertiary level of education, and delivered training workshops both in China and the UK.
